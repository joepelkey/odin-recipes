Brownies

Descripton

This is an easy recipe for brownies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup of your favorite chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 8x8 pan.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter. Add the melted butter to a large bowl with the sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  3. After it's well combined, mix in the flour, cocoa powder, salt, baking powder, and chocolate chips.
  4. Spread the batter into the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Allow them to cool for about 10 minutes before devouring!
