This is an easy recipe for soft chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 125g butter
  • 50g Brown Sugar
  • 50g easter sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 225g flour
  • 1/3 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 300g of chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper
  2. Beat the butter and both types of sugar together using an electric whisk. Add the egg and vanilla and beat some more.
  3. Sift in the flour, baking powder, and slat. Combine all ingredients.
  4. Stir in the chocolate chips
  5. Divide the mixture into 24 balls of dough and arrange them on the baking trays, leaving plenty of space between them.
  6. Bake for around 10 minutes util they are only slightely brown. Leave to cool and enjoy!
